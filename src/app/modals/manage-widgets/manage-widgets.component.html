Manage overview widgets
Balance overview

Display your wallet balances breakdown. Shows current public, blind, anon and staking balances.

Seller's overview

Provides a quick glance at your market listings and associated orders.

Recent transactions

Lists your most recent transaction (both incoming, outgoing and staked).

Buyer's overview

Quick overview of your active orders, that you're buying from others.

Cold staking

Control and monitor your cold staking node. Allows enabling/disabling cold staking and shows current progress towards cold staking.

Staking statistics

Displays current network staking stats, your staking weight and estimates next staking reward.
