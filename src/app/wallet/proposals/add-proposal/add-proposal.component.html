Every Particl user can submit new proposals to Particl network.
As a part of community governance, anybody can post new proposals (or polls). Community initiatives, suggestions for features, in short: anything Particl related.

RE-WORD! Keep in mind, that each Proposal costs 10 PART (paid back to the network) and its results are not obligatory for development of Particl.
